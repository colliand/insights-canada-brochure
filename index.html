Is your business ready for eureka?

Solutions Canada matches businesses and their researchers to outstanding mathematicians, statisticians, computer scientists, and their students at universities across the country. We reach out to businesses and non-profits that seek expertise in the quantitative sciences to advance the success of their organization's operations. Solutions Canada is a joint venture of five mathematical and statistical sciences institutes in Canada. The mission of these institutes is to promote research, training and collaboration in the mathematical and statistical sciences, including applications for industrial innovation.
